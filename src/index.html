What is a CryptoRose?

CryptoRoses are encrypted romantic notes that are dedicated to love ones and permanently stored on Ethereum. They are a symbol of eternal love as they can never be lost or removed from the Blockchain.

How to Dedicate a CryptoRose

Before we can be Romeo and Juliet, we need to do a quick setup so that we can interact with the Blockchain.

1. Obtain some Ether

Ether is a cryptocurrency that one requires to make transactions on the Blockchain. You can buy some from an exchange online.

2. Install MetaMask Extention

MetaMask allows you to interact with decentralised applications using your web browser. Download it before proceeding.

3. Think of Someone Special

Now you are ready to dedicate a CryptoRose to your lover.

The most romantic thing you can do

Romance is all about making an extravagant gesture for someone special to communicate how you feel towards them. CryptoRoses incorporate all the dimensions of traditional romantic gestures.

APPRECIATION

CryptoRoses are gifts that you dedicate to a person to show that you cherish them.

UNBREAKABLE

Just like Love Locks on bridges, CryptoRoses are locked with a private encryption key that can never be broken.

VALUABLE

CryptoRoses are expensive, and so if you receive one, it means that you have someone who admires you.

PERMANENT

Couples love to engrave their initials on trees; now they can do the same with the Blockchain.

PERSONAL

CryptoRoses allow you to add your own love note. Since your key is unique, no two CryptoRoses will be the same
